Self & Art

As a team our aim was to create an editorial online product within one of the themes of the book “Art as Therapy” by Alain De Bottonand John Armstrong. This book introduces a new method of intrepeting art where you use art as a form of therapy. It describes different areas of life which you can battle or handle better with the help of art. The focus areas consisted of love, work, anxiety, nature, politics, self and money. Our team’s focus was on Self with which we created an online magazine called Self & Art.
