Process Work

Redesign of thefork's mobile application.
Thefork is an online restaurant booking application which offers discounts and other services related to booking a restaurant table digitally. The aim of this redesign was to make it easy for the user to quickly book a table and to assist the user to find their preferred choices of place, taste and atmosphere as smoothly and quickly as possible. In the process of work benchmarking, workshops, user researches in forms of interviews, user tests, personas, ideating, user flows and specifications as well as prototyping was performed.
