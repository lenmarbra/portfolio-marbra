At my internship at Sheldon Studio* I worked on a project for their customer Slow News., in which the brief and the mission was to redesign their digital platform. Slow news is among the winner of the Innovative journaslism fund promoted by the European Journalism Centre. As a part of the International slow journalism movement, the three owners and journalists, are promoting values and actions towards a more conscious and slow journalism. They are against the toxic news mainstream, and are instead offering their customers highly validated stories and information which the readers can follow up in forms of series.
