Digital art and design

Magis' customization tool

Our mission of this project was to make Magis digital approach more attractive and aligned with their business approach and identity. Magis is a company that have been creating design products for people's homes, offices and public spaces for more than 40 years. It was founded 1976 in Northern Italy and have throughout the years become an eminent global player within the field. High creativity, quality, and excellence are words describing the company well. As our main improvement proposal we focused on a digitalized customization tool for the furniture.
