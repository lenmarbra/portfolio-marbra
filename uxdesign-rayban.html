UX design

Prescription flow
for RayBan

The aim of this project was to improve the user experience of buying a pair of glasses with a prescription on RayBan's website. Previously, it was difficult for the user to understand the different steps of the flow. RayBan also wanted to be able to provide the customers with feedback regarding availability of glasses based on the information provided in their prescriptions. To the right you'll see a new, improved prescription flow where all the above requirements are met. The design of visuals and layout are following Rayban's current line of design to maintain the site's overall standardizations.
